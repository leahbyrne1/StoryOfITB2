Institute of Technology Blanchardstown

Institute of Technology, Blanchardstown (ITB) was established in 1999, is a third-level institution, and a future constituent institute of Technological University Dublin. ITB provides full-time and part-time higher education courses in a wide range of subjects, making awards at levels from higher certificate, to PhD.
